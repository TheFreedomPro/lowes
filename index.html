WELCOME TO THE AURA CLUB

The only 5-YEAR Purchase Power Agreement To Own

Aura starts as a 5-year power purchase agreement (PPA). You pay for the solar power your roof produces, typically at a lower rate than your utility. After 5 years, the system transitions so you can own it.

  • Lower upfront cost compared to traditional ownership
  • Short 5-year term designed to move you into ownership
  • Program structure applies available commercial-style incentives on the backend
  • Target benefit up to ~35% in incentives when you qualify

Incentives and eligibility vary by home, utility, and program requirements. This is not tax advice.
